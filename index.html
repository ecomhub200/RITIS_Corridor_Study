How the calculation works
  1. Corridor travel time — segment travel times are summed per 15-minute interval (scaled if some segments lack data).
  2. Free-flow reference — 25th percentile of each segment’s night-time travel time, summed over the corridor (the "All Nights 25th % Night" file); INRIX reference speed as fallback.
  3. Delay — actual minus free-flow, for the selected weekdays during AM / Mid-day / PM.
  4. Normalize — median delay (sec) and delay IQR (min), pooled over both directions and divided by the number of signals.
  5. Score — NormDelay × NormIQR × √(AADT ÷ 1,000) × √T, exactly the OCRA spreadsheet formula.
  6. Speed percentiles — corridor space-mean speeds (miles ÷ travel time) per 15-min interval; 15th/50th/85th/95th percentiles for screening and before/after comparison. Per-segment percentiles available as a CSV download.
